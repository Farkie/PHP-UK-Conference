ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

PHP London are pleased to announce the 8th Annual PHP UK conference. For the second time, the conference will run over two days with four great tracks In 2013 the conference will be at the Brewery, in the heart of the City of London.

PHP UK conferences are held in London at the end of February each year. Attended by hundreds of delegates, speakers, sponsors, partners they are run by volunteer members of the PHP London community.

Delegates are typically professional web developers and managers, along with some general PHP enthusiasts and employment recruiters. The event comprises a mix of split-track talks, keynote presentations, panel sessions, unconference slots along with exhibition space for sponsors. Networking is encouraged through informal events and evening socials.

Costs are minimised to keep ticket prices as low as possible. The aim is not to make a profit, but if there are any surplus funds these are used to fund community events throughout the year or possibly to fund the following years conference.
